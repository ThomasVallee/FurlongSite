What Furlong does

Everything your
stable needs.

Paper logs, WhatsApp threads, and memory aren't a system. Furlong gives your team one place to see every horse, every day.

Horse Profiles
Complete digital record for every horse — pedigree, ownership, registration, photos, and the full history a vet or buyer would ever need.
Health Logs
Log vet visits, medications, injuries, and vaccinations in seconds from your phone. Attach photos. Export PDF summaries before appointments.
Training Sessions
Record every work — distance, surface, time, jockey, conditions. Track trends across weeks and spot the pattern before your trainer does.

Owner billing

Stop building
invoices in
Microsoft Word.

If you manage horses with partners, you know the pain. Every month: pull up Word, manually calculate each owner's share of feed, training, vet bills, farrier, labor — then email it and hope they pay.

Furlong automates the whole process. Log expenses as they happen. At month end, hit Generate — and get a professional PDF statement for every owner, split to the exact percentage they own.

Log expenses by category — daily care, labor, training, farrier, vet, meds, entry fees, jockey fees, and more

Register unlimited partners per horse with their exact ownership percentage — handles 2% minority shares or 80% majority stakes equally

One tap generates professional PDF statements for all owners — with race winnings automatically credited back against their share

Track paid vs unpaid statements — payment processing link sharing coming soon

What's coming

The roadmap.

We're building Furlong in public. Here's what's shipped, what's in progress, and where we're headed — updated as things change.

Shipped
Core

Health & vet records

Every vet visit, medication course, and injury on record. Follow-up reminders, severity tracking, attachment storage for X-rays and docs.

Core

Training logs

Workouts, breezes, and gallops with time, distance, surface, effort rating, and Beyer figures. Full career history per horse.

Core

Race record

Full race history per horse. Career W‑P‑S record, earnings, Beyer average, and best surface — calculated automatically.

Pro

Owner billing — automated

Log expenses throughout the month. One tap generates professional PDF statements for every partner, split by exact ownership percentage.

Pro

AI injury risk scoring

Analyzes training load and health patterns. Flags overtraining signals in plain English before they cost you a race.

Pro

AI race entry advisor

Describe an upcoming race. The AI analyses the fit against your horse's form, surface preference, and Beyer range.

Pro

AI peak form forecast

8-week rolling training load analysis. Shows when a horse is building toward a peak, at peak, or being rested.

In progress
Pro

Vet report PDF

One-tap full health history document. Medications, injuries, vet visits with dates — formatted for when a horse ships to a new barn.

Pro

Owner updates

Weekly auto-generated summary email per horse. Workouts, health highlights, upcoming race targets. One tap to send to your ownership group.

Pro

Vaccination calendar

Track vaccines and worming per horse. Push reminders before due dates. Never miss a Coggins or booster again.

Pro

Team roles & permissions

Grooms log, trainers approve. Owners read-only. Unlimited team members on a Pro stable — everyone sees what they need.

Pro

Owner portal

A read-only web link for each ownership partner. They see their horse's records and statements without needing app access.

Pro

Sale prep report

One-page horse summary for buyers. Pedigree, race record, best works, health history — formatted for a sale or private transaction.

Pro

CSV data export

Export all expenses, race results, and training data. For your accountant, your analyst, or your own spreadsheets.
