Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your Furlong account and all associated data, send us an email and we'll take care of it.

support@getfurlong.com

How it works

  1. Send an email to support@getfurlong.com from the email address associated with your Furlong account with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  2. We'll verify your identity and confirm receipt within 2 business days.
  3. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  1. Your account and profile information
  2. All horse records, training logs, and health data
  3. Billing records and owner statements
  4. AI insights and generated reports
  5. Team memberships and stable associations

If you have an active Pro subscription, make sure to cancel it through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store before requesting deletion to avoid further charges.

For questions about your data, see our Privacy Policy.
